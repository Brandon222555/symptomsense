About SymptomSense

A free, private, offline-capable self-diagnostic tool.

🔒 Your data stays on your device

SymptomSense runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your symptom log is stored locally in your browser's storage and never leaves your device.

🧬 How it works

The app matches your selected symptoms against a built-in database of 30+ common conditions. Each condition has a weighted symptom list — the more your symptoms overlap, the higher it ranks. Duration and age group adjust severity scores.

🚑 Severity levels

Manage at home — Mild symptoms likely from a common, self-limiting illness.

See a doctor soon — Symptoms warrant professional evaluation within 1–2 days.

Seek emergency care — Symptoms could indicate a serious or life-threatening condition.

⚠️ Important disclaimer

This app does not replace a doctor. It cannot account for your full medical history, perform physical exams, or run tests. Use it as a first step to understand possible causes — then speak with a licensed healthcare professional.
